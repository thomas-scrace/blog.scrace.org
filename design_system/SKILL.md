## The brand in one breath

*Ink & parchment.* A leather-bound book read by lamplight. Warm near-black
ground, parchment-cream serif type (Georgia — **web-safe fonts only, never load
a webfont**), one restrained gilt-gold accent used like gold leaf. Calm fades,
no bounce, hairlines before boxes, air before chrome. Everything serves one
goal: **make it a delight to read.**

## Non-negotiables

- Serif (Georgia) for everything that is read; small tracked uppercase sans only
  for eyebrows/meta/tags. No webfonts.
- One accent (gilt). Terracotta/sage only for rare semantic marks.
- No emoji. Icons are typographic (fleuron, arrows, curly quotes) or thin Lucide.
- Sentence case in prose & headings. First person, essayistic, unhurried.
- Reading-first layout: ~66ch measure, 19px/1.72 body, generous whitespace.
